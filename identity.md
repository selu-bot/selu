# Selu - Brand Identity

## Name
**Selu** (selu.bot)

## What is Selu?
Personal Agent Platform - a platform that makes personal AI agents accessible to everyone.

## Name Origins & Associations
- Soft, warm, globally pronounceable (4 letters)
- Cherokee: "Corn Mother" - figure of nourishment, wisdom, care (note: sacred cultural figure - be mindful, don't reference mythology directly in branding)
- ML/AI: SELU = Scaled Exponential Linear Unit (activation function) - subtle tech credibility in uppercase
- Samoan: "comb" (neutral)
- No negative connotations found in any major language

## Brand Personality
- **Warm, not techy** - Selu feels like a companion, not a tool
- **Guiding** - lights the way, helps you navigate
- **Approachable** - designed for people who aren't tech-savvy
- **Personal** - your agent, your way
- **Calm & capable** - gets things done without overwhelming you

## Target Audience
- Users who want a personal assistant/agent
- Not necessarily tech-savvy
- Value simplicity and ease of use

## Tone of Voice
- Friendly, clear, jargon-free
- Helpful without being patronizing
- Confident but not loud
- Human-first language

## Visual Direction (Ideas)
- Soft, rounded typography
- Warm color palette (think: golden light, soft blues, warm neutrals)
- Simple, clean iconography
- Possible logomark directions:
  - Abstract "S" as a flowing path/guide
  - Gentle orb/light motif
  - Minimalist companion figure

## Domain
- **Primary:** selu.bot
- **Alternatives to secure if available:**
  - selu.app
  - selu.ai
  - getselu.com

## Tagline Ideas
- "Your personal agent"
- "Selu gets it done"
- "The easiest way to get things done"
- "Your helpful companion"
- "Simply handled"
