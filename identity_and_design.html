Guidelines

Usage Rules

Keep the brand consistent. These rules apply to all uses of the Selu logo and character.

Do

  • Use the full character at sizes > 80px
  • Use the face-only variant for app icons and favicons
  • Pair character + wordmark for header logos
  • Maintain 15% padding around the character
  • Use on white, dark, or gradient backgrounds
  • Animate the character in onboarding and notifications

Don't

  • Stretch or distort the character
  • Change the eye direction
  • Remove the blush or smile
  • Place on busy or patterned backgrounds
  • Use the character below 24px (use wordmark alone)
  • Reference Cherokee mythology in branding
