## Name

**Selu** — 4 letters, soft, warm, globally pronounceable.

### Meanings across languages

| Language | Meaning | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Cherokee | Corn Mother (ᏎᎷ) | Sacred feminine figure of nourishment, wisdom, sacrifice. Do NOT reference mythology in branding. |
| Samoan | Comb | Neutral |
| Tetum / Fataluku | To pay | Neutral |
| ML/AI | Scaled Exponential Linear Unit | Activation function — subtle tech credibility in uppercase |

No negative connotations found in any major language.

### Cultural sensitivity note
The Cherokee Corn Mother is a sacred cultural figure. The brand does not reference or leverage this mythology. The name was chosen independently for its sound and feel.

## Tone of Voice

- Friendly, clear, jargon-free
- Helpful without being patronizing
- Confident but not loud
- Human-first language
- Uses simple words over complex ones
- Okay to be playful — never corporate

### Examples

| Instead of | Say |
|------------|-----|
| "Configure your agent parameters" | "Set up your agent" |
| "Authentication failed" | "Couldn't sign you in — try again?" |

## Logo: The Companion

The Selu logo is a **character mascot** — a friendly, rounded creature with expressive eyes, rosy cheeks, and a gentle smile. It creates instant emotional connection and works across all touchpoints.

Think: Duolingo's owl, Discord's Clyde, Reddit's Snoo — but warmer and softer.

### Character anatomy

- **Body**: Circle, blush-to-coral gradient (#FF7EB3 → #FF6B5A)
- **Ears**: Two small circles above the body, same gradient, partially overlapped by body
- **Eyes**: White ellipses with navy (#2D2B55) pupils and white highlights (looking slightly up-right — attentive, optimistic)
- **Blush**: Subtle pink ellipses on cheeks (#FF4070, 20% opacity)
- **Smile**: Simple quadratic curve, navy stroke, rounded caps

### Logo variants

| Variant | Usage |
|---------|-------|
| **Full character** | Marketing, website hero, print, large formats |
| **Face-only (on gradient bg)** | App icon, favicon, compact spaces |
| **White silhouette** | On colored backgrounds, overlays |
| **Character + wordmark** | Header logos, social media profiles |

### Wordmark

- Font: **Nunito Extra Bold / Black** (900 weight)
- Case: **all lowercase** — `selu`
- Optional accent dot: `selu.` with dot in Coral (#FF6B5A)
- Letter-spacing: -0.5px (slightly tight)

## Usage Guidelines

### Do

- Use the full character at large sizes (> 80px)
- Use the face-only version for app icons and favicons
- Pair the character with the wordmark for header logos
- Maintain padding around the character (at least 15% of character width)
- Use the character on white, dark, or gradient backgrounds

### Don't

- Don't stretch or distort the character
- Don't change the eye direction (it's part of the personality)
- Don't remove the blush or smile
- Don't place on busy/patterned backgrounds
- Don't use the character at less than 24px (use wordmark alone at tiny sizes)
- Don't reference Cherokee mythology in any branding material

## Mascot Personality (for UI/Marketing copy)

The Selu companion is:
- **Helpful** — always ready, never pushy
- **Curious** — the upward-looking eyes suggest eagerness to learn your needs
- **Cheerful** — the smile and blush radiate warmth
- **Quiet confidence** — it doesn't shout, it just handles things

Use the character in:
- Onboarding flows (waving, pointing, celebrating)
- Empty states ("I'm ready when you are")
- Notifications (peeking in from the corner)
- Error screens (apologetic expression variant)
- Marketing (stickers, social media, merch)
